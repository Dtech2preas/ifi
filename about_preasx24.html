About The Architect (PREASX24)

Jonas Mochebane, operating under the alias PREASX24, is a South African Independent Software Architect and CEO. Over the last 8 years, he has independently engineered and deployed an expansive 18-project digital infrastructure known as The D-TECH Ecosystem.

Jonas specializes in resource-efficient engineering—bypassing heavy, expensive cloud infrastructures by mastering serverless Edge computing (Cloudflare Workers), WebRTC Peer-to-Peer tunneling, and advanced Android native-to-JS bridges. His work proves that enterprise-grade, scalable software can be built on a solo budget through high-level technical resourcefulness.

While a significant portion of his portfolio is dedicated to providing free, low-data EdTech platforms for South African students (driven by his own experiences at Nelson Mandela University), his engineering breadth extends into Financial Algorithmic Trading (MQL5), custom Android Background Services, and complex Virtual Economies.
